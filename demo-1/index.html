Global Warming

The phenomenon of global warming is when radiation emitted by the sun is absorbed by the earth. This radiation absorbed by the earth is then emitted back outwards causing the surface of the earth to warm. When the radiation emitted by earth tries to go back to space, it is trapped by greenhouse gasses in earth's atmosphere that in turn send radiation back to earth's surface causing it to perpetually warm.
